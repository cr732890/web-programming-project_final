    About V-Labs+

    Open science education for every student

    V-Labs+ is a free virtual laboratory platform built for Indian school students, providing 14 safe and interactive science experiments aligned with the NCERT curriculum, backed by a full REST API.

    🎯

    Our Mission

    Democratise practical science education in India — every student, regardless of geography or resources, gets access to high-quality virtual experiments.

    ⚙️

    Tech Stack

    Frontend: Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS with Canvas-based simulations. Backend: Node.js + Express + MongoDB with JWT auth, Mongoose models, and full REST API.

    📖

    NCERT Aligned

    All 14 labs mapped to Class 9–12 science + maths syllabi. Use V-Labs+ as a supplement to your school practicals before board exams.

    🌐

    Open Source

    Inspired by the OLabs initiative. All frontend content is freely accessible without login. Backend code is fully documented and deployable on any Node.js host.
